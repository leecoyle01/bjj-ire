"What is BJJ?"

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (BJJ) is a grappling based martial art with ties to Traditional Japanese Jiu Jitsu, Judo, submission wrestling and more. Developed by the Gracie family in Brazil, it has now become world renowned for its effectiveness and is now taught and practiced in Ireland by blackbelt instructors and highly competitive training partners with great camaraderie.
